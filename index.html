How to Play Stockle

Guess the secret stock ticker in 6 tries!

  • Enter a valid stock ticker of the required length and press Enter or Guess.
  • The TradingView chart above shows the price history of the secret ticker (with its name hidden!) - use it as a clue.
  • After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was.

Examples:

AAPL - Letter A is correct.

MSFT - Letter S is present.

GOOG - Letter O is absent.

Use the Freebie button once per game for a hint!
